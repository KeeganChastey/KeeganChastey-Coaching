My Coaching Career

Experience

Personal Sessions

I have been providing personal one-on-one and group coaching for the past two years for local players. It is something that I really enjoy and I believe has a lot of value to my players. I have worked with kids between ages 6 and 15 and can adjust my sessions to make the most out of each player's skill level. This is something I really enjoy and I would love to gain some more clients!

Gitchi Gummi Soccer Club

Through Gitch Soccer, I have had the opportunity to be the Assistant Coach to both Morgan McClean and my dad, Barry Chastey. I have learned a lot by learning from two of the top coaches in the area. I was Mo’s assistant for the U14 boys team that was promoted to Premier last year and helped out with my dad’s U18 Premier boys team. I have also been a part of the Youth Development Program and a coach for the Indoor Soccer League in Proctor.

My Lfie and Soccer

In addition to these more formal coaching experiences, the thing that contributed most to my soccer knowledge is the fact that I have been around the game my whole life. From watching my dad’s CSS team to the countless hours on the couch watching Premier League soccer, I have been building my knowledge all my life. I hope to share some of that with the people I coach and pass on the love for the game.
